## **1\. Purpose & Scope**

This document defines **what aspects mean** in Cloudia’s system.

Aspects are treated as:

* Interaction patterns  
* Feedback loops  
* Tension dynamics  
* Information exchange pathways

They are **not**:

* Good or bad omens  
* Predictors of success or failure  
* Permanent personality judgments

This framework replaces binary aspect logic with **adaptive systems thinking**.

---

## **2\. Global Aspect Principles (Non-Negotiable)**

### **2.1 Aspects Describe Relationships Between Functions**

An aspect describes:

* How two psychological functions interact  
* The quality of feedback between them  
* Where attention, friction, or amplification occurs

An aspect does **not** describe:

* External events  
* Moral outcomes  
* Personal worth

---

### **2.2 No Aspect Is Inherently Positive or Negative**

All aspects contain:

* Productive expressions  
* Distorted expressions  
* Latent potential

Ease can stagnate.  
Friction can catalyze growth.

Language implying benefit or harm is prohibited.

---

### **2.3 Context Always Overrides Aspect Type**

Aspect meaning is **never** interpreted without:

* The planets involved  
* The sign environments  
* The temporal context  
* The broader configuration

Aspect type alone is insufficient.

## **3\. Canonical Aspect Types (Modern Interpretation)**

Each aspect below is defined as a **system behavior**, not a value judgment.

---

## **☌ Conjunction — Amplification & Fusion**

### **Core Dynamic**

* Two functions occupy the same attentional space  
* Energy is intensified and concentrated

### **Experiential Quality**

* Focus  
* Saturation  
* Heightened salience

### **Constructive Expression**

* Strong coherence  
* Unified drive  
* Deep focus

### **Distorted Expression**

* Over-identification  
* Blind spots due to intensity  
* Burnout through excess

### **Interpretive Language**

* “Amplifies”  
* “Intensifies”  
* “Brings into sharp focus”

### **Language to Avoid**

* “Overwhelming”  
* “Too much energy”  
* “Fated union”

## **4\. Aspect Intensity Modifiers**

Aspect experience may be shaped by:

* Orb tightness  
* Repetition across configurations  
* Angularity  
* Lunar phase activation  
* Ongoing arcs

Intensity is framed as:

* Increased awareness  
* Not increased consequence

---

## **5\. Multi-Aspect Interpretation Rules**

When multiple aspects are present:

* Do not rank aspects by “strength” alone  
* Look for recurring themes  
* Identify dominant interaction patterns  
* Allow multiple interpretations to coexist

Single-aspect storytelling is prohibited.

---

## **6\. Anti-Repetition & Rotation Guidance**

Cloudia must:

* Rotate metaphors per aspect type  
* Vary interpretive lenses (psychological, relational, systems)  
* Avoid repeating “tension / ease” binaries

Example:  
❌ “This square creates tension”  
✅ “This interaction may require ongoing adjustment as competing priorities surface”
